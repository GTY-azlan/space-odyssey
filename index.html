Historic Missions

Milestones of Space Exploration

Six decades of pushing the boundaries of what's possible

1957

Sputnik 1

The first artificial satellite launched by the Soviet Union, igniting the Space Race.

1961

Vostok 1

Yuri Gagarin becomes the first human to orbit Earth—a 108-minute flight that changed history.

1969

Apollo 11

Neil Armstrong & Buzz Aldrin walk on the Moon. 600 million people watch live on TV.

1990

Hubble Telescope

Launched into orbit, Hubble revealed the universe in ways never seen before.

2012

Mars Rover

Curiosity lands on Mars, beginning years of exploration on the Red Planet.

2021

James Webb

The most powerful space telescope ever built, peering into the dawn of time.
